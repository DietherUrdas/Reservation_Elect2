About Us

Established in 1995, Darrel & Ayien's Five Star Hotel has been a beacon of luxury and excellence in the hospitality industry. Named after our founders, Diether Urdas and Andrei Saromo, our hotel represents a perfect blend of timeless elegance and modern sophistication.

With over 200 elegantly designed rooms and suites, we have been serving distinguished guests from around the world for nearly three decades. Our commitment to exceptional service and attention to detail has earned us numerous awards and recognition in the hospitality sector.

Our Mission

To provide unparalleled luxury experiences that exceed our guests' expectations while maintaining the highest standards of service, comfort, and hospitality. We strive to create memorable moments that last a lifetime.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier destination for luxury hospitality, setting new benchmarks in the industry through innovation, sustainability, and unwavering dedication to guest satisfaction.
